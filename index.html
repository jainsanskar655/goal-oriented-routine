Sanskar's 6-Month Goal-Oriented Routine

My Schedule

Morning Routine (6:00 AM – 9:00 AM)

  • Wake Up and Hydrate (6:00–6:10 AM): Start the day with a glass of water and light stretches.
  • Japanese Learning (6:10–7:00 AM): Focus on vocabulary, grammar, or listening using apps and textbooks.
  • Breakfast and Cooking Prep (7:00–8:00 AM): Prepare a nutritious breakfast and lunch for work if needed.
  • Personal Development or Light Reading (8:00–8:30 AM): Read a book or listen to a podcast for growth.
  • Get Ready for Work (8:30–9:00 AM): Prepare for the workday.
  • Commute to Office (9:00–10:00 AM): Relax or listen to educational content while commuting.

Office Hours (10:00 AM – 6:00 PM)

  • Morning Focus (10:00 AM–1:00 PM): Work on high-priority tasks.
  • Lunch Break (1:00–1:45 PM): Enjoy a healthy, light meal to recharge.
  • Afternoon Work (2:00–6:00 PM): Meetings, client interactions, and routine tasks.

Evening Routine (6:00 PM – 10:30 PM)

  • Gym Workout (6:00–7:00 PM): Focus on strength training or cardio.
  • Commute Home and Refresh (7:00–7:30 PM): Travel back home and freshen up.
  • Dinner Prep and Eating (7:30–8:15 PM): Cook and enjoy a balanced meal.
  • Competitive Exam Preparation (8:15–9:15 PM): Focus on structured study for exams.
  • Japanese Listening Practice (9:15–9:45 PM): Watch Japanese shows or practice listening skills.
  • Wind Down and Reflection (9:45–10:15 PM): Journal, engage in mindfulness or gratitude meditation.
  • Bedtime Prep (10:15–10:30 PM): Get ready for bed.
  • Sleep (10:30 PM): Aim for 7.5+ hours of quality sleep.
